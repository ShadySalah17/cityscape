Real Estate Funds

They are investment funds that aim to own and develop lands, establish residential, commercial, office, logistical and other units, in addition to acquiring developed real estate that generates income. The fund aims to achieve profits for investors through this.

Financing Funds

Investment funds established for the purpose of engaging in financing activities, by providing financing to companies in various sectors, with the aim of achieving profits for investors by distributing them periodically to unit holders.
